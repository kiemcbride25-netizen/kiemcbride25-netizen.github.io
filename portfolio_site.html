Why a Roth IRA?
✓
Tax-free compounding
All growth and withdrawals after 59½ are completely tax-free. At age 18-19, I'm in the lowest tax bracket I'll ever be — the best time to fund a Roth.
✓
40+ year horizon
At 7% average real return, $1,000 today becomes ~$15,000 at retirement. Every dollar I add compounds tax-free for decades.
✓
Forces long-term thinking
The account structure discourages speculation and encourages the buy-and-hold discipline central to sound wealth management.

How to Build a Strong Thesis
1
Read the 10-K & latest earnings call
Know the business model, revenue drivers, and what management is actually saying. Ask Claude to summarize any 10-K.
2
Identify what the market is missing
Good theses aren't "great company" — they're "the market underestimates X because of Y, and here's when it will be proven wrong."
3
Steel-man the bear case
The best investors know the risks better than the bulls. Being able to articulate the bear case and explain why you own it anyway = intellectual honesty.
4
Write it down, date it
A dated thesis you can walk into an interview with is orders of magnitude more impressive than verbal stock picks. This is your proof of process.
